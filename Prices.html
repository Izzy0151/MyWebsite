MORE INFO

We deliver around Manchester with a 10 mile radius. Costs for deliveries between 0 to 5 mile radius are £2.30 extra any delivery distances over 5 miles to 10 cost £2.60. Deliveries usually take around 1 - 2 Hours depending on the type of dessert and distance. We dont deliver outside that given range. Any orders over £20, the delivery will be free!

Please make sure you enter the correct desired amount if you're using the custom amounts as we sometimes get customers who make mistakes and enter the wrong amount and we'd need to call them to double check that everything is correct!

If you prefer collection instead of delivery, please confirm your order or call us to order and your order should be ready within 30 - 45 minutes and can be collected instore.

If you have been in our store before and we gave you a voucher, please call us and dwe will apply the voucher to your order.
