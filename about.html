About Us

So who are we?

Ursula - Founder

Ursula founded this company since 1987 and ever since it had been doing astoundingly. Ursula started baking and making deserts since the age of 15. At the age of 28 she had already opened up her Dessert Shop. She travelled the world to taste different kinds of desserts and find out what other cultures had to offer. By that time she had also come up with multiple of her own made recipes.

Ursula's Dessert Company is already planning to open multiple stores around UK with a chance of opening more in different countries!

The team has since grew with more members of staff on hand selling you some of the best desserts from around the world in one place!
